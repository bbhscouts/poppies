Paracord Poppy

Navigate between slides with the arrows in the bottom right

Equipment

Required:

75cm Red Paracord

75cm Green Paracord

50cm Black Paracord

Sharp Scissors

Lighter or Lit Tealight

Optional:

Hot glue gun

Excess Paracord

Use a sharp pair of scissors to cut the paracord and then apply direct heat (from your lighter or tealight) to the exposed end to melt it and stop it from fraying.

Assembly

You should now have your three separate knots. If you haven't already trim off the excess paracord now.

Use the arrows to go down to see the final assembly steps

Stem + Petals

Pull the Stem through the centre of the Petals. Then either use a dab of hot glue, or melt the paracord to secure the stem in place.

Flower + Leaf

Arrange the Leaf at an angle behind the flower. Then either use a dab of hot glue, or melt the paracord to secure the Leaf in place.

Congratulations!

You've completed your poppy!
